OUR PROJECTS

DAINTREE RAINFOREST

With your help we are working towards buying back property in the irreplaceable and complex Daintree Rainforest, the oldest rainforest in the world and also one of the most biologically diverse.

RAINFOREST NURSERY

Our plant nursery located in the Daintree National park is the source of all revegetation and rainforest restoration projects in the Daintree. The nursery produces over 20000 trees and plants every year.

PROTECT WILDLIFE

We have partnered with government organisations, Aboriginal organisations and universities in order to raise awareness of endangered species in rainforests, particularly in the Daintree.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

GENERAL DONATION

By making a general donation of any amount, you will be supporting us in protecting and preserving rainforests and their wildlife all across the globe, as well as funding several of our major projects.

MONTHLY DONATION

In becoming a monthly supporter you join our Rainforest Guardian program, which ensures we can continue to make great conservation efforts for the future of rainforests and their species.

BUY BACK RAINFOREST

By making a donation to the Buy Back program, you are raising funds for us to purchase rainforest. This will help to conserve these areas of forest forever, ensuring that it is protected for future generations to experience.
